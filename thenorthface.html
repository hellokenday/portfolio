The North Face

Introduction

Basecamp is a custom dashboard built for the Claromentis intranet platform. It was my job to gather requirements, produce wireframe layouts and mockups, work with stakeholders to action design feedback, and develop the interface to replace an outdated system.

Layout

The North Face needed a responsive dashboard to keep track of seasonal product releases and deadlines. They experimented with social components for team communication but found they were not adopted effectively. We kept things simple, including components that would help with workflow and not distract users from finding relevant information, such as sales dates or product catalogues.

Branding

Taking their brand guidelines and adapting them to a style guide I was able to make the intranet complement The North Face brand.

The theme throughout was for users to feel they are at base camp, where they can find everything they need to push ahead with their tasks. This was achieved by using a mountaineering style background image, brand accent colours for secondary interface elements, and by keeping elements grouped by task.
